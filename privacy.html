Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Multi Cloud Fusion collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our website, mobile apps, and cloud-connected features.

Multi Cloud Fusion is operated by an independent developer as a sole proprietor (not a separate incorporated legal entity).

Information We Collect

  • Account details from connected providers, such as account ID, email, and profile display name.
  • Connection credentials, including OAuth tokens for Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, and Dropbox. For MEGA (when enabled), account credentials may be stored locally in secure device storage.
  • Cloud file metadata and file content required to run sync and backup features that you choose to enable.
  • App configuration data, such as selected folders, sync mode, schedules, provider preferences, and local app logs.
  • Purchase and entitlement data from Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat when billing features are used.
  • Technical and usage data needed to operate, secure, and improve the service.

OAuth Providers and Requested Scopes

When you connect cloud providers, we use OAuth where available. Requested scopes are limited to the features used in the app and may vary by app version or enabled provider settings.

  • Google Drive: drive.file, drive.appdata, and drive.metadata.readonly.
  • Microsoft OneDrive: openid, profile, offline_access, User.Read, Files.Read, and Files.ReadWrite.
  • Dropbox: files.metadata.read, files.content.read, files.content.write, and account_info.read.

Google API Services User Data

The use and transfer of information received from Google APIs by Multi Cloud Fusion adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Google user data is used only to provide user-facing app functionality. It is not sold, not used for advertising or generalized AI/ML model training, and not transferred except as needed to operate the feature you requested.

Device Permissions

The app may request file/storage permission to let you choose local folders for backup and notification permission to show sync status and background job updates.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate linked accounts and perform sync, backup, restore, and file-management operations requested by you.
  • To support background sync jobs and account-level storage usage calculations.
  • To provide support, troubleshoot issues, and respond to legal or privacy requests.
  • To process purchases and entitlement checks for premium features.
  • To improve reliability, security, and product quality.

Analytics and Product Metrics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) for website analytics. In-app analytics uses PostHog and is controlled by an in-app preference.

Where required by law, non-essential analytics is enabled only with consent. You can disable in-app analytics in settings and manage web analytics through cookie or browser controls.

Data Sharing and Processors

We do not sell personal information. We share data only as needed to provide the service with providers such as Google, Microsoft, Dropbox, MEGA integration services, PostHog, RevenueCat, and app store billing platforms.

Connected cloud content is not used for advertising or data brokerage. Data may be disclosed when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and service integrity.

Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens and sensitive credentials are stored using device secure storage. App settings and operational metadata may be stored locally on device and, when enabled, app-state backup data may be stored in connected cloud app data locations.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service operation, legal obligations, and security purposes. When you remove a linked account, we remove stored credentials for that account from the app.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 (or older where required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can request access, correction, deletion, or export of your personal information. You can also revoke provider access from your Google, Microsoft, or Dropbox account settings and disable in-app analytics.

Data Deletion Instructions

You can review account and data deletion steps at our Data Deletion page, including provider revocation steps and account deletion requests.

Contact

Questions about this policy or data deletion requests can be sent to contact@gtlabs72.net.
